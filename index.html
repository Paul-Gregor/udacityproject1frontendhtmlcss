Tourbillion

In watchmaking, a tourbillon, also called a “rotating cage”,
is a horological complication, added to the escapement mechanism,
intended to improve the precision of mechanical watches by counterbalancing the disturbances of the isochronism of the resonator due to terrestrial gravity.
